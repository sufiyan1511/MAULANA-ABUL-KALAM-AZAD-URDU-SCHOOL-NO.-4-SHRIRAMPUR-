About Us

MAULANA ABUL KALAM AZAD URDU SCHOOL NO. 4 ,SHRIRAMPUR is an Urdu Medium school recognized by the Government of Maharashtra and run by the Muncipal Co-orporation, Shrirampur. It is a Muslim Minority Institution.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to provide a happy, caring and stimulating environment where children will recognize and achieve their fullest potential, So that they can make their best contribution to the society.
